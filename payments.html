Payment FAQs

How long does it take for my payment to be processed?

Bank transfers and mobile money payments are typically processed within 24 hours of receiving your payment confirmation. PayPal payments are usually processed instantly.

Is it safe to pay with my credit card on your website?

Yes, all credit card transactions are processed through secure payment gateways with encryption to protect your information.

Do you accept payments in other currencies?

Yes, we accept USD, EUR, and GBP through PayPal. The amount will be converted to GHS at the current exchange rate.

What should I do if my payment fails?

If your payment fails, please try again or use an alternative payment method. If the problem persists, contact our support team at payments@foursmartbrothers.com.

Can I get a receipt for my payment?

Yes, we provide receipts for all payments. You will receive an email receipt within 24 hours of your payment being processed.
