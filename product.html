Product Description

Our Men's African Print Shirt is a celebration of African culture and contemporary fashion. Each shirt is carefully crafted by our skilled tailors using premium quality Ankara fabric sourced directly from local markets.

The shirt features a classic button-down design with a regular collar, short sleeves, and a straight hem. The vibrant colors and intricate patterns make this shirt stand out while maintaining a professional look suitable for both casual and semi-formal occasions.

We offer standard and slim fit options to ensure the perfect fit for every body type. All our shirts are pre-washed to prevent shrinkage and maintain color vibrancy after multiple washes.
